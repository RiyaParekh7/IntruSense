Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about IntruSense

What is IntruSense?

AI-powered security platform that monitors user activity and detects potential insider threats by analyzing behavior patterns in real time.

How does IntruSense detect threats?

Uses machine learning algorithms to identify unusual behavior such as irregular login times, abnormal data transfers, or suspicious activity patterns.

What is an Insider Threat?

Risk posed by individuals within an organization (employees or contractors) who may misuse their access to sensitive data.

What is the ITS Score?

ITS (Insider Threat Score) is a numerical value indicating the level of risk associated with a user's activity. Higher scores = higher risk.

Does IntruSense monitor users in real time?

Yes, continuously analyzes activity and updates alerts and dashboards dynamically to reflect real-time behavior.

Is my data secure with IntruSense?

Yes, designed with security in mind. Focuses on detecting threats without exposing sensitive information unnecessarily.

Do I need technical knowledge to use this platform?

No, dashboard is simple and user-friendly for both technical and non-technical users.
